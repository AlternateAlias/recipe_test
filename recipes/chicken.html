Chicken Fried Chicken Recipe

Description of Recipe:

This recipe will require 30 saltine crackers, 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour 2 tablespoons of dry potato flakes, 1 teaspoon seasoned salt, 1 and a half teaspoons of ground black pepper one egg, six skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, and 2 cups of vegetable oil for frying.

First, you place the crackers in a large plastic bag, seal the bag, and then crush the crackers with a rolling pin until they are crumbs (not too fine, just enough to where they're in chunks but broken anyway). Then, add the flour, potato flakes, salt/pepper and mix. Second, you beat the egg until it is a liquid yolk. Coat the cricken pieces in this egg, one by one, and then place in crumb mixture. Seal said bag and shake it until all pieces are coated. Third, heat oil in a deep-fryer or saucepan of choice to 350 degrees Fahrenheit, or 175 Degrees Celsius. Lastly, fry the chicken (turning in random intervals to heat both sides) until it is a golden brown color. Drain the juices after, and enjoy!
  • 30 Saltine Crackers
  • 2 Tbsps of all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tbsps of dry potato flakes
  • 1 Tspn of seasoned salt
  • 1-and-one-half tspns of ground black pepper
  • One egg
  • Six skinless/boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups of Vegetable Oil
  1. Place Crackers in plastic bag and crush them into coarse bits.
  2. Beat Egg to liquid yolk and coat chicken. Seal after.
  3. Heat oil in deep-fryer or Saucepan to 350 Fahrenheit
  4. Fry chicken, turning when necessary, to golden brown color
  5. Enjoy!
